# Privacy Policy for SupraWall Security Dify Plugin

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

SupraWall is committed to protecting your privacy while providing advanced security for AI agents. This policy explains how our Dify plugin handles data.

## 1. Data Collection
The SupraWall Dify Plugin is designed to be a local-first bridge.
- **Inputs**: The plugin processes the text content you send for security scanning (e.g., tool calls, agent outputs).
- **API Keys**: Your SupraWall API key is stored securely within Dify's secret management and is never transmitted to third parties other than the official SupraWall API (`api.supra-wall.com`).

## 2. Data Usage
Data is exclusively used for:
- **Security Evaluation**: Detecting prompt injection, SQL injection, and PII.
- **Policy Enforcement**: Checking if an action matches your configured security rules.
- **Audit Logging**: Logs are stored in your own SupraWall dashboard for your forensic review.

## 3. Data Storage
- **We do not retain permanent copies** of your scanned content on our evaluation servers unless you have enabled specific forensic logging in your dashboard.
- All transmissions are encrypted via industry-standard TLS.

## 4. Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

## 5. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@supra-wall.com or visit https://supra-wall.com.
